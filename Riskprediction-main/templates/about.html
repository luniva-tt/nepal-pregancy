About MamuCare

MamuCare is a state-of-the-art maternal health monitoring system designed to reduce pregnancy risks through AI-driven insights.

Our Mission

To provide every mother in Nepal with accessible, high-accuracy health screening tools, bridging the gap between rural healthcare and modern medical technology.

AI Technology

Our ensemble learning model analyzes vital health markers to predict risk levels with high precision, helping healthcare providers intervene early when necessary.
